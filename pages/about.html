Who We Are

The Community Empowerment and Advocacy Network (CEAN) is a non-profit organization established to respond to systemic social, economic, and environmental challenges affecting communities across Kenya.

CEAN adopts a rights-based, community-centered approach that empowers individuals and groups to actively participate in shaping their own development.

Our Vision

A just, inclusive, and empowered society where all people enjoy dignity, equal opportunity, and sustainable livelihoods.

Our Mission

To empower communities through advocacy, economic development, gender equality, environmental stewardship, and access to health, education, and justice.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Transparency, accountability, and ethical leadership.
  • Inclusivity: Respect for diversity and equal participation.
  • Empowerment: Strengthening communities to drive their own development.
  • Compassion: People-centered and rights-based approaches.
  • Sustainability: Long-term solutions for lasting impact.
  • Collaboration: Partnerships that amplify results.
