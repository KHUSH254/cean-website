Our Programs

CEAN designs and implements integrated programs that respond to community priorities while addressing structural barriers to development.

Scholarships, mentorship, digital literacy, and leadership training.

Health education, awareness, and improved access to essential services.

Gender Equality & GBV Prevention

Advocacy, prevention initiatives, and survivor-centered support.

Entrepreneurship development, savings groups, and income generation.

Environment & Climate Action

Environmental conservation, tree planting, and climate resilience.

Youth, Peace & Leadership

Leadership development, civic engagement, and peacebuilding.
