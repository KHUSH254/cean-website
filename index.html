Who We Are

The Community Empowerment and Advocacy Network (CEAN) is a Kenyan-based non-profit organization dedicated to addressing systemic social, economic, and environmental challenges affecting vulnerable and underserved communities.

Our Focus Areas

Education & Skills Development

Promoting access to quality education, life skills, and youth leadership.

Health & Reproductive Rights

Advancing community health, dignity, and informed reproductive choices.

Gender Equality & GBV Prevention

Advocating for safety, equity, and justice for women and marginalized groups.

Economic Empowerment

Strengthening livelihoods through entrepreneurship and financial inclusion.

Environment & Climate Action

Promoting conservation, climate resilience, and sustainable practices.

Youth, Peace & Leadership

Building responsible leadership and peaceful, cohesive communities.

Our Commitment

Guided by integrity, inclusivity, and sustainability, CEAN works in partnership with communities, government institutions, civil society, and development partners to deliver lasting and measurable impact across Kenya.
